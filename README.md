# Root Out - AI-Powered Weed Removal Robot

## 🌱 Project Overview

Root Out is an innovative agricultural technology solution that leverages artificial intelligence and robotics to provide efficient, eco-friendly weed removal for farms, vineyards, orchards, and gardens. By automating weed detection and removal, Root Out reduces the need for harmful chemical herbicides while improving agricultural productivity.

## 🎯 Unique Value Proposition

**Weeds are left on the spot to decompose into natural manure.**

Root Out offers an automatic AI robot for efficient and fast removal of weeds, resulting in:
- ✅ Reduced use of harmful chemicals
- ✅ Natural composting on-site
- ✅ Improved soil health
- ✅ Cost-effective weed management
- ✅ Sustainable agricultural practices
